About CodeWave

CodeWave is software company established in 2001 in Wroclaw, Poland. Starting with two co-founders, currently we are employing more than a dozen of highly qualified software developers. We have successfully released more than 40 custom development projects for international and Polish partners. Our experienced and creative team has never lost the youth spirit. We have been specialised in all kinds of web development- starting from simple web apps up to advanced and highly efficient B2B platforms. Our strongest areas of expertise are database performance optimisation, Flash and HTML5 based video streaming and effective mass mailing solutions. We do love new, demanding challenges!
